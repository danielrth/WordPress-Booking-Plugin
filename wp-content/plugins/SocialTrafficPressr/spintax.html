How to use spintax:

Spintax (for our purposes) is simply putting special characters around words so that the software will randomly rotate through them.
 
Example: Let’s say that you are posting a comment and you want to cycle through synonyms that express how much you like the post..
 
All you do is put them in Spintax and the software will randomly rotate through the words and pick a random one each time it posts.
 

How is it done

It’s simple, you simply put { before all the information, put } at the end of the information, and put  |  in between each piece of information. Confusing I know so I will demonstrate.
 
Words to spin:
Cool
Awesome
Amazing
Incredible
 
In spintax they look like:
 
{Cool|Awesome|Amazing|Incredible}
